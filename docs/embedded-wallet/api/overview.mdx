## Overview

Our server-side SDKs can act as a companion to our clients to help perform [certain functions](/docs/embedded-wallet/api/server-side-sdks/overview#use-cases) on your back-end.

To utilize any of Magic’s SDKs, you will need to sign up on our [developer dashboard](https://dashboard.magic.link/) to create an app and get keys. Take a look at the [quickstart](/docs/embedded-wallet/quickstart/overview) for a step by step guide.
