## Overview

For simplicity, Magic is the default identity provider for authentication. For increased control over user authentication and identity, you can [bring your own IDP](docs/authentication/login/idp) and use JWTs to securely integrate with Magic. Authentication method availability and scope will depend on your [wallet type](/docs/wallets/wallet-types#overview).
