## Usage

### Prerequisites

* You will need a [Facebook Developer](https://developers.facebook.com/) account
* You will need to have the [Magic SDK installed](/embedded-wallets/sdk/overview) into your web app
* You will need to have the [Magic SDK - OAuth Extension](/embedded-wallets/authentication/login/oauth/oauth-implementation) installed into your web app

### Facebook Setup

After installing the OAuth extension, you can now enable Facebook Login for your Magic application:

1.  Follow Facebook's instructions to [register a Facebook app](https://developers.facebook.com/docs/apps#register)
2.  When creating your Facebook app, select **Authenticate and request data from users with Facebook Login**, click Next and follow the prompts to add app information

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/facebook-step-1.webp" alt="facebook-step-1.png"/>
</Frame>

3.  On the next screen, you'll be presented with steps to customize your Facebook app. Click **Customize adding a Facebook Login button**
<Frame>
  <img src="/images/facebook-step-2.webp" alt="Facebook app customization screen with Login button option"/>
</Frame>

4.  Go to your [Magic Dashboard](https://dashboard.magic.link/login)
5.  Select the Magic app for which you'd like to enable Facebook Login, or create a new app
6.  Navigate to **Authenticate -> Social Login** from the sidebar
7.  Click the toggle for **Facebook**
8.  Back in your Facebook Login Settings, add your **Redirect URI** in the **Valid OAuth Redirect URIs** input:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/facebook-step-4.webp" alt="Facebook OAuth settings showing Valid OAuth Redirect URIs input field"/>
</Frame>

    Add the **Redirect URI** you are passing as the **redirectURI** argument to the **loginWithRedirect** method:
    
    ```javascript JavaScript icon="square-js"
    await magic.oauth2.loginWithRedirect({
      provider: 'facebook',
      redirectURI: 'https://your-app.com/your/oauth/callback', // whitelist this with Facebook
    });
    ```

9.  Save the changes in your Facebook Login Settings
10. From the main Facebook dashboard, click on **App Settings -> Basic**
11. Copy the **App Id** and **App Secret**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/facebook-step-5.webp" alt="Facebook App Settings showing App ID and App Secret credentials"/>
</Frame>

12. Return to your Magic Dashboard and input the **App Id** and **App Secret** for your Facebook OAuth app
13. In Magic Dashboard, click **Save**
